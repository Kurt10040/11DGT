Rankings

TETR.IO Ranks

The TETR.IO ranks are as follows: ?, D, D+, C-, C, C+, B-, B, B+, A-, A, A+, S-, S, S+, SS, U, X, X+

For eaach rank in TETR.IO there is a TR requirement to be able to get that rank.

TR: Stands for Tetrio Rating. It's used to determine your tetrio rank and is accumulated from Tetrio league wins.

Tetra League

Tetra league is the ranked or competetive mode of TETR.IO in which you battle against other players to win TR and climb the ranks. You are paired with a player of similar rank to play against for a number of ranks.

When starting out a player will be unranked (?). To gain a rank you must play 10 placement games so that the TETR.IO ranking system can determine the correct rank to put you in.

To win a match it is first to 3 for A+ and below, 5 rounds for SS and below, and 7 rounds for U and above.
